## Full Description (Copy-Paste to Creem)

### Transform your YouTube research into actionable notes.

ObsidiTube Pro is the ultimate companion for students, researchers, and power-users who use Obsidian or Notion to manage their learning. While the free version helps you get started, **ObsidiTube Pro** removes all limits and provides the deep data you need for a professional second brain.

#### 🚀 What you unlock with Pro:

*   **Unlimited Everything:** Convert playlists of any length. No more 10-video limits—whether it's a 20-video course or a 200-video archive, we handle it all.
*   **Rich Metadata:** Every card automatically includes **Duration**, **View Count**, and **Publish Date**. Perfect for prioritizing your "To Watch" list.
*   **Obsidian Properties (YAML):** Your generated files now start with full YAML frontmatter, including playlist descriptions, total views, and source links.
*   **Advanced Exports:** Unlocks high-fidelity **CSV** and **JSON** exports for data analysis or custom database imports.
*   **Lifetime Access:** Pay once, own it forever. All future updates and parsing improvements are included.

#### 🛡️ Privacy First
ObsidiTube processes your data in-memory and never stores your playlist URLs or generated cards. Your research stays your own.
